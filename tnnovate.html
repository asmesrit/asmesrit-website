About ASME Tamil Nadu Section

Established on January 23, 2024, the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) Tamil Nadu Section serves as a vibrant hub for mechanical engineering professionals and students across the state.

Our mission is to create engaging pathways for professional members and graduate students, expand the ASME professional community, and facilitate the development, dissemination, and application of cutting-edge engineering knowledge.

About ASME SRIT

The ASME Student Section at Sri Ramakrishna Institute of Technology was established in 2022 and has quickly become a hub of engineering enthusiasm and innovation on campus.

In the ASME Competition leaderboard for 2022-2023, Sri Ramakrishna Institute of Technology secured the First position overall, demonstrating our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Event Vision

The ASME TN-NOVATE: SDG Innovation Expo 2025 empowers students to develop creative solutions to pressing global issues through Sustainable Development Goals, fostering widespread engagement and exposure to diverse ideas nationally.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that all people enjoy peace and prosperity by 2030.
