Recognition & Achievements

Selected for Pre-Finals of Shine Healthcare Hackathon

Aug 2025: The Team Gix Notion has selected for the Pre-Finals round of the Shine Healthcare Hackathon jointly organized by The Tamilnadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, StartupTN and Aakam360. The Pre-finals happened at UEI Coimbatore Cohort and the team presented the project on "Bongos Therapy for Autism Children"

Best Home in Town - Senior Citizens Home Visit

July 2025: Our members visited the Senior Citizens Home in Mathipalayam, Thenkarai, Coimbatore, spending a day sharing smiles, stories, and heartfelt moments with the residents. Nearly 25 students came together to bring warmth, wisdom, and joy, making it a truly memorable experience for everyone involved.

February 4-7, 2025: Team Garudans represented ASME_SRIT at the Tamil Nadu Karting Championship (TNKC) - Season 2, organized by Mechnido in collaboration with PPG Institute of Technology, Coimbatore.
